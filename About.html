Brief History, Mission, and Values

Golf le Fleur*, created by Tyler, the Creator in 2017,
began as a bold, vibrant sneaker line with Converse.It
evolved into a lifestyle brand blending streetwear
and high fashion. Our mission is to push creative
boundaries while embracing inclusivity,
sustainability, and values artistic expression and
excellence in every piece, whether it’s clothing or
accessories.

Inspirations

From blending vibrant streetwear aesthetics with
high fashion, driven by a love for bold self-expression
and modern, functional design. We aim to create
unique pieces that reflect creativity, individuality,
and effortless style.

Achievements

Golf le Fleur* has achieved major milestones,
including collaborations with Converse, exclusive
fashion drops, and pop-up shops, gaining recognition
for its bold, artistic designs.

Why People Chose Us

People choose Golf le Fleur* for its unique blend of
streetwear and high fashion, bold creativity, and
Tyler, the Creator's visionary touch. The brand stands
out for its distinctive style, high-quality materials,
and innovative designs.
